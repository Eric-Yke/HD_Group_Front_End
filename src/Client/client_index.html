Welcome to Housing Service Pro!
A public platform that connects local housing service specialists with people in need.

What services do we provide?

The platform offers a variety of housing services, such as:
  • Tree removal
  • Roof cleaning
  • Fence installation
  • Oven repairs
  • Weeding
  • etc.

How do I start using the serive?

To start using the service, click User Center and fill up your personal details.

How do I pay for the service?

As a client, you can either pay per service use or you can pay a fixed membership fee annually and are entitled to unlimited assistance callouts.

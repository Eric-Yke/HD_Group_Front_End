  • Roof cleaning
  • Fence installation
  • Oven repairs
  • etc.

How do I start using the serive?

To start using the service, click User Center and fill up your personal details.

How do I pay for the serive?

As a client, you can either pay per service use or you can pay a fixed membership fee annually and are entitled to unlimited assistance callouts.

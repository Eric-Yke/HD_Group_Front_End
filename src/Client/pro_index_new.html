Welcome to Housing Service Pro!
A public platform that connects local housing service specialists with people in need.

What services do we need form you?

The platform offers a variety of housing services, such as:
  • Tree removal
  • Roof cleaning
  • Fence installation
  • Oven repairs
  • Weeding
  • etc.
Thus, we need you to have at least one of these skills above to offer service to clients.

How do I start using the service?

To start using the service, click User Center and fill up your personal details.
After that, as a service provider, you must register for joining the membership and pay a fixed membership fee annually before you starting provide service to clients.
Meanwhile, a certain amount of commission feee will be charged when the job is completed and the transaction is done.
